Church Accessibility Self-Evaluation


Instructions

Fill out the form below to assess your church’s accessibility for people with disabilities. The form is anonymous except for asking for your church’s name. The church name field is included in case you would like to include your church’s name in the results (for such purposes as printed or sending to other members of your church). You may leave this field blank if you wish.

After providing demographic information, you will be asked yes/no questions in the following 6 areas: Awareness, Physical Building, Community Outreach, Music and Multimedia, Resources and Curriculum, and Events. Please answer all questions to the best of your ability. When the results are tabulated and displayed, unanswered yes/no questions will be treated as if they were answered 'no.'
